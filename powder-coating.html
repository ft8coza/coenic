At COENIC, we understand that sandblasting is only the beginning. To truly protect and elevate your metal assets, our powder coating service provides a thick, resilient barrier that standard paint simply cannot match.

By applying electrostatic charged powder to a perfectly prepared sandblasted surface, we ensure a molecular bond that withstands the harsh Cape Town coastal environment, preventing rust and mechanical wear.

Unmatched Versatility

Versatile Powder Coating for a
Wide Range of Applications

Powder coating is renowned for its exceptional versatility, offering a superior finish for a vast array of metal items. Through an advanced electrostatic process, the coating bonds molecularly to the metal surface, creating a resilient, long-lasting barrier. This makes it the preferred choice for everything from delicate residential architectural features to heavy-duty industrial machinery, ensuring both aesthetic beauty and structural integrity in any environment.

Our Precision Process

The journey from raw metal to a flawless finish.

01
Sandblasting

Stripping away rust and old layers to create a mechanical profile for bonding.

02
Cleaning

Removing oils and chemical residues to ensure the powder adheres perfectly.

03
Application

Electrostatic spraying of powder to ensure even coverage in every crevice.

04
Curing

Baking the parts in industrial ovens to fuse the powder into a hard shell.

05
Quality Check

Rigorous inspection for thickness, adhesion, and aesthetic perfection.
