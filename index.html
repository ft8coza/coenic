Residential surfaces

From restoring historical facades to renewing outdoor spaces, we bring precision and care to every residential project.

  • Exterior Walls: Remove old paint, stains, or other contaminants from exterior walls, revealing a clean and refreshed surface.
  • Wooden Decks and Fences: Sandblasting can help strip away old finishes, paint, or weathered layers from wooden decks and fences, preparing them for refinishing
  • Brick Surfaces: Bricks on the exterior or interior of a residential property can benefit from sandblasting to remove dirt, grime, and old paint, restoring their original appearance.
  • Metal Gates and Railings: Sandblasting is effective for removing rust, paint, and corrosion from metal gates and railings, enhancing both aesthetics and durability.
  • Concrete Driveways and Walkways: Sandblasting can be used to clean and rejuvenate concrete surfaces, removing stains, paint, or other blemishes for a renewed look.
  • Stone Pavers and Pathways: Sandblasting can help restore the natural beauty of stone pavers and pathways by removing accumulated dirt, moss, or old sealant.
  • Pool Areas: Sandblasting is useful for preparing pool areas by removing old coatings, paint, or stains from surfaces around the pool.
  • Metal Furniture: Patio furniture made of metal, such as wrought iron, can be sandblasted to remove rust and old finishes, providing a smooth surface for refinishing.
  • Wooden Surfaces: Wooden elements like doors, shutters, or wooden trim can benefit from sandblasting to remove old paint or finishes, preparing them for a fresh look.

Automobile

  • Automobile Bodies: Remove old paint, rust, and surface imperfections from automobile bodies, preparing them for repainting or refinishing.
  • Rims and Wheels: Strip away old coatings, corrosion, and brake dust from rims and wheels, enhancing their appearance and longevity.
  • Chassis and Frame: The chassis and frame of automobiles can benefit from sandblasting to remove rust and corrosion, ensuring structural integrity and extending the lifespan.
  • Engine Components: Sandblasting is used to clean engine components, removing grease, grime, and paint, creating a clean surface for maintenance or restoration work.
  • Undercarriage: The undercarriage of a vehicle can be sandblasted to remove road debris, rust, and contaminants, helping to prevent corrosion and maintain the overall condition.
  • Truck Beds: Sandblasting is an effective method for stripping old paint, rust, and coatings from truck beds, preparing them for protective coatings or bed liners.
  • Interior Parts: Certain interior parts, such as metal dashboards or door panels, can be sandblasted to remove old paint or finishes, providing a smooth surface for refinishing.
  • Exhaust Systems: Clean and remove rust from exhaust systems, helping to maintain proper function and extend the life of these components.
  • Classic Car Restoration: Restoration of classic cars, helping to strip away layers of paint and rust to reveal the original beauty of the vehicle.
  • Commercial Vehicle Surfaces: Sandblasting is applicable to various surfaces on commercial vehicles, including trailers, buses, and delivery trucks, to remove rust and prepare for protective coatings.

Paving & Buildings

  • Pavements and Sidewalks: Clean and refresh pavements and sidewalks by removing dirt, grime, and weathered surface layers, enhancing the overall appearance.
  • Building Facades: Cleaning and restoring building facades, removing pollutants, paint, and stains, revealing the original beauty of the structure.
  • Road Markings and Signs: Remove old road markings, paint, and reflective materials from roads and signs, providing a clean slate for new markings.
  • Building Facades: Cleaning and restoring building facades, removing pollutants, paint, and stains, revealing the original beauty of the structure.
  • Highways and Bridges: Maintain the integrity of highways and bridges by removing rust, corrosion, and old coatings, ensuring a durable and safe infrastructure.
  • Airport Runways: Maintaining airport runways, removing rubber buildup from landing gear and enhancing overall safety for aircraft.
  • Historical Monuments: Preservation of historical monuments, gently removing dirt, pollutants, and aging layers to reveal the original detailing.

Roads, Bridges & Tunnels

  • Highways and Bridges: Sandblasting helps maintain the integrity of highways and bridges by removing rust, corrosion, and old coatings, ensuring a durable and safe infrastructure.
  • Road Markings and Signs: Sandblasting is used to remove old road markings, paint, and reflective materials from roads and signs, providing a clean slate for new markings.
  • Parking Lots: Sandblasting is utilized to refresh parking lots by removing old paint, markings, and oil stains, creating a clean and well-defined space for vehicles.
  • Airport Runways: Sandblasting is essential for maintaining airport runways, removing rubber buildup from landing gear and enhancing overall safety for aircraft.
  • Railroad Tracks and Platforms: Sandblasting is applied to clean and prepare railroad tracks and platforms, removing rust and contaminants to ensure smooth and safe operations.
  • Historical Monuments: Sandblasting is often used in the preservation of historical monuments, gently removing dirt, pollutants, and aging layers to reveal the original detailing.
  • Airport Tarmacs: Sandblasting is crucial for maintaining airport tarmacs, removing rubber deposits and contaminants to ensure optimal friction and safety during takeoffs and landings.
